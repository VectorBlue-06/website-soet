Residential Life

A Home Away
From Home

Samrat Vikramaditya Vishwavidyalaya provides well-maintained, secure hostel facilities for both boys and girls — designed to foster academic focus, personal growth, and a vibrant community spirit.

Spread across multiple residential blocks, each hostel is overseen by a dedicated warden and supported by round-the-clock security, modern amenities, and hygienic, nutritious dining facilities.

Campus Dining

Mess & Cafeteria Facilities

Four separate dining facilities ensure every student — resident or day-scholar — has access to nutritious, hygienic, and affordable meals throughout the day.

Central Boys Mess

A large-capacity dining hall serving pure vegetarian, nutritionally balanced meals for all male residents. A rotating weekly menu ensures variety, and a Mess Committee of student representatives oversees quality.

Breakfast 7:00–8:30 Lunch 12:00–2:00 Dinner 7:00–9:00

Vidyottama Girls Mess

An exclusive dining hall for female residents, with a focus on hygiene, balanced diets, and seasonal variety. Access is restricted to hostel residents and authorised visitors only.

Breakfast 7:00–8:30 Lunch 12:00–2:00 Dinner 7:00–9:00

Cooperative Mess

A student-managed facility where residents collaboratively plan the weekly menu, manage procurement, and review quality — a practical exercise in budgeting, teamwork, and institutional responsibility.

Breakfast 7:00–9:00 Lunch 12:00–2:30 Dinner 7:30–9:30

Quick Bite Cafeteria

A daytime facility open to all students and visitors, offering snacks, beverages, and light meals. Conveniently located near the academic blocks, it is a popular gathering spot between lectures.

Open 8:00 AM–8:00 PM All Students

Hostel Rules

Guidelines &
Important Rules

  • Residents must return to hostel by 10:00 PM on weekdays.
  • Guests are not permitted inside rooms without prior warden approval.
  • Use of alcohol, tobacco, or illegal substances is strictly prohibited.
  • Ragging in any form will result in immediate expulsion from the hostel.
  • Residents are responsible for the upkeep and cleanliness of their rooms.
  • Cooking in rooms is not permitted; use designated mess/cafeteria only.
  • Hostel fee must be paid before the deadline each semester to retain the allotment.
  • Noise after 11:00 PM is prohibited to maintain a conducive study environment.
