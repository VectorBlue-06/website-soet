Dean's Message

Shaping Engineers,
Nurturing Leaders.

Welcome to the School of Engineering and Technology! The Office of the Dean (Student Welfare) is dedicated to ensuring that your time at the university is enriching, safe, and holistic. We believe that true engineering excellence is achieved not just inside the classroom, but through active participation in campus life, leadership roles, and community service.

Our office serves as the central hub for all student activities, grievance redressal, extracurricular clubs, and personal counseling. We are here to support your mental, physical, and academic well-being. My door is always open to students who need guidance, have innovative ideas for the campus, or require support navigating university life.

Wishing you a transformative journey at SOET — build boldly, think deeply, and serve generously.

What We Do

Student Services
& Support Pillars

The Dean's Office manages a wide array of student-centric initiatives — from disciplinary oversight and wellness to scholarship facilitation and hostel governance.

Discipline & Anti-Ragging

Maintaining a zero-tolerance policy against ragging and ensuring a safe, respectful campus environment for all students through regular awareness drives and vigilance committees.

Know more

Clubs & Societies

Overseeing the formation, funding, and events of all technical, cultural, and sports clubs managed by the Student Council — over 18 active bodies year-round.

Explore clubs

Counseling & Wellness

Providing confidential psychological counseling and peer mentorship to help students manage academic pressure, career anxiety, and personal well-being effectively.

Reach out

Scholarships & Approvals

Facilitating state and central government scholarship verifications, bonafide certificates, student NOCs, and financial aid processing on behalf of enrolled students.

Learn more

Hostel & Mess Welfare

Coordinating with Chief Wardens to ensure quality living conditions, hygiene standards, and proper dining facilities across all university hostels and residential blocks.

View facilities

Grievance Redressal

A structured committee and accessible portal to transparently address student complaints regarding academics, infrastructure, peer disputes, or administrative matters.
